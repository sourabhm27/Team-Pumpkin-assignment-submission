About the Logo

Concept & rationale

The name NOURA is inspired by the word "nourish," representing health, vitality, and natural goodness. It reflects the brand's purpose of nourishing the body with fresh fruit juices made from quality ingredients — a name that's short, modern, easy to pronounce, and memorable, making it suitable for a contemporary FMCG brand. NOURA is positioned as a premium yet approachable choice for people with active lifestyles, built around real fruit goodness, freshness, and everyday wellness, with a mission to make healthy hydration simple, enjoyable, and accessible.

The brand is built on the values of freshness, purity, and sustainability. It encourages consumers to choose natural ingredients over artificial alternatives while promoting a healthier lifestyle, aiming to build trust by delivering quality products made with care and respect for nature.

The visual identity reflects these values through a clean and memorable design. The custom "U" is transformed into a glass of orange juice, symbolizing freshness and nourishment, while the orange slice and green leaf represent natural ingredients and farm-fresh quality. The combination of warm orange and natural green reinforces energy, health, and freshness, creating a friendly and recognizable brand identity.
